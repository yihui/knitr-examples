Another approach was mentioned in FAQ 9 (\url{https://github.com/yihui/knitr/blob/master/FAQ.md}), i.e. destroy the chunk header. Here is an example:

\begin{verbatim}
<<test_label, echo=1:2>>=
1 + 1
rnorm(10)
2 + 2
@
\end{verbatim}
\end{document}
